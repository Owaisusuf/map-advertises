What is Tohfaa?

Tohfaa is the merchandise wing of MAP Advertisers. We specialize in custom-printed gifts, corporate merchandise, and personalized keepsakes — crafted with premium quality and delivered with love.

Fully Custom

Bring any design, text, logo or custom idea to life. We print exactly what you envision for any occasion.

Premium Quality

Utilizing high-end professional-grade machinery, premium inks, and rigorous quality check routines.

Fast Delivery

Prompt turnaround times with local delivery options across Srinagar and Kashmir, ready for gifting.
